About Eva

Trust the Process

Hi, I'm Eva Kothari — a pro certified bridal makeup artist based in Ahmedabad, Gujarat. Makeup has always been my language of expression; I speak it fluently on every face I touch.

I took a big leap and moved to Mumbai on my own to train at the prestigious Bombay School of Makeup and Hair (BSMH) — my first time away from family, but every moment was worth it. That experience shaped not just my skills, but my courage.

When I'm not transforming faces, you'll find me exploring new cities, discovering local cafés ☕, or planning my next food adventure. I believe a well-travelled soul creates more soulful art.
